Our Mission

At HackathonNet, we're dedicated to creating a platform that brings together talented individuals, mentors, and sponsors to foster innovation and technological advancement. We believe in the power of collaboration and the potential of every participant to make a difference.

Our Story

We are a group of ambitious cloud engineering students from ESPRIT University, based in Tunisia. Our journey started not in a garage, but in a noisy cafeteria where dreams were louder than the espresso machine.

Driven by caffeine, curiosity, and a few bugs that we now call "features," we decided to build something that would leave a mark on the tech world â€” or at least our final year project jury.

Since then, we've pretended to launch over 50 fake startups, crashed more servers than we care to admit, and convinced ChatGPT to write half of our documentation. Despite it all, we remain passionate creators, bonded by our love for the cloud, innovation, and free pizza during hackathons.
